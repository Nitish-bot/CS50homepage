I don't want you to buy,

I want you to think!

why?

Why does every website look like this? It's as if we have cracked some *code* (pun not intended) and we have finally figured out how websites are supposed to look for maximum effiency, whatever that means.

Start with a hook, keep it minimal, don't distract the user, give them social proof UGH! Isn't that all so boring, I want my websites to be a work of art. I want to provide an experience not a means to an end.

I get why one would do that, but can you really enjoy that? Where is the problem solving then? Has it just been reduced to debugging or is it possible to actually deviate and do something different with a website?

I guess we'll never know!
